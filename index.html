ASRTU meetingin Yakutsk -2018

September 13th to 18th, 2018 Yakutsk, Russia

Association of Sino-Russian technical universities (ASRTU) is the leading association establishing and developing cooperation between universities in Russia and China. Events conducted jointly with member universities and observer universities increase the possibilities for implementing joint scientific, technical and innovation projects of universities from both countries.

The 2018 ASRTU Meeting in Yakutsk, Russia will take place from September 13th to 18th, 2018. This conference will further promote cooperation within the framework of the ASRTU through bilateral harmonization between the Permanent Secretariats of the ASRTU from both Russia and China.
